#  Student Mentor Application (LearnAxis)

A modern web application designed to **simplify student information access, strengthen mentor–mentee interaction, and support career growth** — all from a single platform.

 **Live Application:** https://v0-student-mentor-application-two.vercel.app/

---

##  Overview

The **Student Mentor Application (LearnAxis)** is built to address common challenges in academic environments where students struggle to access their information and maintain effective communication with mentors.

This platform provides **dedicated dashboards for students and mentors**, enabling smooth interaction, centralized academic data access, and structured mentorship. A standout feature of the application is the **integration of internship opportunities**, helping students move beyond academics and prepare for real-world careers.

---

##  Core Features

### Student Dashboard
- Easy access to academic information such as:
  - Attendance
  - Fee details
  - Assignments
- Centralized view of all important student-related data
- Direct interaction with assigned mentors

###  Mentor Dashboard
- Manage and monitor assigned students
- Track student progress efficiently
- Guide students through academic and career decisions
- Improve mentorship effectiveness through structured interaction

###  Seamless Mentor–Student Interaction
- Designed to reduce communication gaps
- Encourages clarity, guidance, and continuous support
- Helps build a stronger mentor–mentee relationship

###  Internship Opportunities (Unique Feature)
- Provides students access to internship opportunities directly within the platform
- Bridges the gap between academics and professional exposure
- Encourages career-oriented learning alongside education

### User-Friendly & Accessible
- Clean and intuitive UI
- Responsive design for easy access across devices
- Focused on usability for both students and mentors

---

## Purpose & Impact

The Student Mentor Application aims to:
- Centralize student academic information
- Strengthen mentor–mentee engagement
- Improve accessibility and transparency
- Support students in both **academic growth and career development**

By combining **mentorship, academic tracking, and internship access**, the platform offers a holistic solution for modern educational needs.
